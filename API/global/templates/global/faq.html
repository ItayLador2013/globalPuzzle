What is globalPuzzle?

globalPuzzle is a cutting-edge mobile app designed to seamlessly connect individuals who have relocated to new countries and share similar cultures and backgrounds. Our platform offers a secure and nurturing environment where users can forge meaningful connections with others who truly understand their unique experiences. By facilitating interactions among like-minded individuals, globalPuzzle creates a supportive space for building relationships based on shared cultural affinities in recently relocated individuals.

What is globalPuzzle LLC’s mission?

At globalPuzzle LLC, our mission is to connect individuals who have relocated to new countries, facilitating the formation of friendships, the discovery of supportive communities, and the fostering of a sense of belonging. We believe that regardless of location, everyone deserves to feel connected and valued. Leveraging advanced technology and a comprehensive dataset, our user-friendly platform matches individuals based on cultural backgrounds, shared experiences, and personal preferences. By bridging the gap between newcomers and local communities, we enable individuals to build lasting relationships with like-minded people who understand their challenges and share similar cultural references. Through our commitment to innovation and inclusivity, we strive to be the premier platform for anyone seeking friendship, community, and a sense of belonging in their new country.

How does the matching process work?

globalPuzzle employs advanced probability-based artificial intelligence- made entirely by us- to facilitate meaningful connections among individuals who have recently relocated to a different country, with similar cultures and backgrounds. Our AI system utilizes a comprehensive dataset to assess the likelihood of cultural similarity based on users' country of origin and other factors as such. We maintain strict standards, allowing only users with a high matching probability to be included in the list of potential connections. By analyzing user-provided information, such as stated hobbies, we determine compatibility and generate a ranked list of the top 12 matches. To promote diverse cultural experiences, our platform limits the inclusion of users from the same country to a maximum of four out of the twelve matches. At globalPuzzle, our aim is to foster connections that enrich cross-cultural interactions and create meaningful relationships.

How to avoid phishing?

While we understand that it is impossible to completely eliminate the risk of phishing, we prioritize your safety and recommend taking certain precautions when engaging with other users on our platform. It is crucial not to disclose any personal information, including addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses. Additionally, we urge you to remain vigilant for any suspicious activity during chats or conversations and promptly report any concerning behavior. To enhance security, we strongly encourage utilizing the video calling feature as much as possible instead of relying solely on text messaging. By adhering to these guidelines, you can maximize your safety while enjoying meaningful connections on globalPuzzle.

Why use the in-app messenger rather than phone or any other messengers?

At globalPuzzle, we place the utmost emphasis on safety and privacy, and we are dedicated to providing you with the best possible user experience. In order to ensure your safety and maintain a high level of privacy, we STRONGLY recommend utilizing ONLY the in-app chat and calling services. By using these built-in features, you can communicate and connect with others seamlessly, without the need to share personal information such as phone numbers or email addresses. This practice aligns with our commitment to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your interactions remain secure and meaningful. By adhering to these guidelines and relying solely on our in-app services, you can confidently enjoy a safe and enriching experience on globalPuzzle.

How to change personal information?

To provide you with full control over your personal information, GlobalPuzzle offers a convenient and user-friendly option to edit any details you have provided during profile creation. Simply navigate to the settings page within the app by tapping the "≡" icon when viewing your profile. From there, you can effortlessly modify or delete any information as desired, ensuring your profile accurately reflects your preferences and evolving needs. Once you have made the desired changes, simply save them, and your updated profile will be securely updated. At globalPuzzle, we prioritize your privacy and strive to empower you with seamless control over your personal information.
